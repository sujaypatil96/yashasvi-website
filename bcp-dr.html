Introduction


Fig.: Business Continuity Planning - Disaster Recovery

Business Continuity (BC) management is a service that provides a framework to ensure the resilience of your business to any eventuality. It ensures continuity of service to your key customers and the protection of your brand and reputation. Corporate governance and industry legislation have made executives liable for the security of their corporate information. It provides a basis for planning to ensure your long-term survivability following a disruptive event including both on-premise and cloud workloads and data. How data is safe in cloud and cloud provider liability associated with data protection and data security.

Deliverables:

Following are the deliverables of this service:

  • Detailed Business Continuity Plan
  • Detailed Disaster Recovery Plans
  • Disaster Recovery drills to test the BCP/DR plans
