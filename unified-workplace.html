  Unified Workplace

This Solution offering can help streamline majority of intricacy involved in managing to end-user needs and hence reducing costs. The Solution is aimed at improving end user efficiency, improves productivity and secured access to services, applications through technologies and our expertise. As part of this service the following areas of end-user computing area is addressed which includes:

  • Asset management
  • Patch management and delivery
  • End User Devices standardization
  • License management
  • Software distribution
  • End point security
  • Device Management
  • End User Device lifecycle management
  • Bring Your Own Device (BYOD)
  • Virtualization of desktop and application

  Mobility

This Enterprise Mobility solutions offering can help streamline majority of complexities involved to gain the productivity, collaboration and innovation of mobility. Our consultative approach will cover network, security and operational excellence to architect the mobility solutions with security framework. This service provides rapid mobility deployment and adoption to enhance the productivity and cost avoidance. The solution covers the Enterprise Mobility Management solutions based on-premise, cloud.

  Workplace Security

This Workplace Security Solution offering can help to protect the workplace by safeguarding the assets and employees against the evolving threats. The continuous safeguarding requires the regular evaluation of tools, technologies, policies and people at right time with right skills. The workplace security encompasses all layers of infrastructure not limiting to end users devices, Network (wire and Wireless), Mobility, and Data Centre.
